After meeting with Kim Jong Un, a few days later I headed down to the southeren border of North Korea also known as the DMZ. This place is the current border between South Korea and North Korea. There is a heavy military presence in the area so we had to be careful. We were and overall it was a very intriguing place to visit.

My adventure at the Ryugyong Hotel

January 5, 2020

Staying within the capital of Pyongyang, I visited the mysetrious Ryugyong Hotel. It is the tallst uncompleted strutcure in the world. It is 1,080ft tall with 105 floors! But, It has never had a single guest. The unique design was one of the various reasons why I wanted to visit it. Even though, it is not a functioning hotel it serves other purposes. Most of these include an amazing backdrop for propaganda messages. Overall, the Ryugyong hotel was a interesting and diverse experience.

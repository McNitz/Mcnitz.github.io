Important Minecraft Mobs

Minecraft Bee. Lives in a bee hive. Honey can be collected if the hive is over a campfire and when the player uses shears.
Minecraft Chicken. Lays eggs and makes noise.
Minecraft Hoglin. Dumb version of big with floppy ears and attacks you in the nether. Scared of mushrooms.
PewdiePie's minecraft skin. Second biggest youtuber with over 100million subs and is an epic gamer.
Minecraft Phantom. Scary flying creature of the night. It only appears if you dont sleep for 3 or more nights. swoops down from above and bites you. Drops phantom membrane which helps you repair you elytra.
Minecraft pig. Makes noises and drops pork chops. You can ride them if you put a sattle on them and use a carrot on a stick.
Minecraft Piglin. They are half pig half man. Live in the nether. Get triggered if you dont wear gold armour. They scam you in trading.
Minecraft Pillager. They are bullies with crowsbows and ride ravagers. They drop emeralds tho.

History

Miami Started out by being inhabited by the native American Tribe "Tequestas" They were in control of Most of Eastern Florida.
When the Europeans came across they had infrequent meetings with the natives. They later migrated out of south East Florida.
Miami become notable when their crops were the only ones to survive after a big freeze during the 1800s. Soon after, a local land owner got a
Rail road owner to invest into Miami therefore making it a city even thought it had a bit over 300 people at the time.Miami saw major growth during the 1920s.
This slowed a bit during the Great depression however. Miami came into importance during World War 2 because of its location in United States.
The last notable event to help boost Miami is when Fidel Castro took over cuba. This made plenty of Cuban Immigrants to come to Miami.
